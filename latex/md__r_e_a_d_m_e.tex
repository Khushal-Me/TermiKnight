{\bfseries{Termi\+Knight}} is a {\bfseries{terminal-\/based}} C++ adventure game set in a {\bfseries{modern-\/day fantasy}} world. Players explore {\bfseries{multiple lands}}, each containing randomly generated structures (caves, castles, or villages) filled with rooms, enemies, and puzzles. The game features {\bfseries{multiple character classes}}, a {\bfseries{turn-\/based combat system}} with {\bfseries{numeric choices}}, {\bfseries{riddle-\/based progression}}, and an {\bfseries{inventory system}} that supports {\bfseries{weapon-\/equipping}} and {\bfseries{potion usage}}. A {\bfseries{\doxylink{class_save_manager}{Save\+Manager}}} automatically persists player progress, ensuring your heroic feats are never lost!\hypertarget{md__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md1}{}\doxysection{\texorpdfstring{{\bfseries{Key Features}}}{{\bfseries{Key Features}}}}\label{md__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md1}

\begin{DoxyItemize}

\item {\bfseries{Luck (LCK)}} – influences random outcomes (flee success, certain skills) ~\newline

\item Blocking halves incoming damage for the next enemy turn. ~\newline

\item Certain class skills increase flee chance (Bandit) or allow double damage (Soldier). ~\newline

\item Enemies scale with your current land or your own level (depending on your design).

\item Weapons, once “used,” boost your Attack stat permanently (or until you replace it).

\item Ultimately, gather all artifacts to break the curse and win the game.
